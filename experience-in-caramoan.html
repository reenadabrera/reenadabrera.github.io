Adventures > Caramoan > Experience in Caramoan

i once live in Caramoan when I was young. My parents are both from there.
I have many relatives form both sides. I have many cousins to play and have a lot of fun.
I live a simple life there. Running around, swimming and exploring the beautiful surroundings of Caramoan.
But sadly, when I was seven years old, my parents brought me to Makati City to continue my studies.
i only got to see Caramoan during summer or christmas season. The time away always makes me excited to go back there.
it is my first home so i really love that place.
When we visit Caramoan, me and my family with go swimming on beautiful beaches and have fun until we got sunburned.
We tend to forgot to bring sunblock causing us to get sunburn and dark skin after each swim. Sunburn can be painful but oh well.
at least we had fun. Now, it has been more than a year since i had been there and i'm really hoping to go back this Christmas.
